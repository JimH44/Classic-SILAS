SCR_old_Styles.txt -- control file for updating style names
in files based on old versions of SCR_Vn.dot or SCR_PN95, so SILAS can format them.

Comment lines have no semi-colons in them.
Change lines have the old stylename that should be replaced first,
then after the semicolon the new replacement name.

Stylenames without a semicolon won't be updated.
